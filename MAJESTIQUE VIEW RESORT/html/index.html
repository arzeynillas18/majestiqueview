SIMPLY AMAZING

Majestique View Resort is your royal paradise, located in Brgy. Binongkalan, Catmon. Easily accessable as it is located on the roadside with an amazing scenery you will not be ready for. Though simple from outside its gates, once you are inside it is another story you have never imagined.

ABOUT US

Most resorts, may it be ones with pools or beaches, restrict when it comes to diving. With Majestique View, restrictions are gone and you can jump as many times as you want and let that adrenaline flow throughout your body with the thrill of doing diving tricks on the diving boards to showcase your skills. The best thing is, you do not have to pay too much for this. Even with a tight budget you can enjoy and destress.
